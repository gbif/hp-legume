We are pleased to share with you **Issue 72** of The Bean Bag!

Issue 72 includes a short history of the 50 years of the Bean Bag, as well as the following updates on legume research and news:

- reports from the Legume Phylogeny Working Group, 
- announcements, 
- paper digests, 
- portraits of two leguminologists, 
- a botanical artist spotlight, 
- new legume species highlights, 
- a photo gallery and 
- the ever increasing annual bibliography of legume literature.

We wish you all a 2026 full of legume research, which we encourage you to share with the legume community in the next issue of the Bean Bag.
